Exide Life Insurance Company Limited, 100 percent owned by Exide Industries Limited, is an established and profitable company that serves over 10 lakh customers in over 200 cities in India. The Company manages over ₹8,800 crores in assets. The company is focused on providing long term protection, savings and investment solutions to help Indians prepare for their long and happy lives.
